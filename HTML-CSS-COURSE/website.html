KATANY YOUTH GROUP

This group was established to unite the community through a youth initiative, from the Kaplelach age-set to the current Nyongi age-set. Our main goal is to elevate our community by focusing on key areas. We welcome opinions from all members, as long as we maintain decorum and adhere to the rules, which will be developed in future meetings and communicated through our WhatsApp forum
